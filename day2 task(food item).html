Tornado potatoes

Ingredients:

  1. Potato
  2. parmesan cheese
  3. peper
  4. garlic cheese
  5. paprika
  6. salt

Prepartion:

Preheat the oven to 3250F.microwave the potatoes for 1-11/2minutes,then leave in the microwave for a few mintues to cool down and soften. insert a wooden skewers into the bottom of each potatos and gently push it all the ways through the top.Working in a spiral motion from one side to the other, hold a sharp, thin knife at an angle and cut in the opposite direction you are turning the skewered potato, cutting all the way down to the skewer. Take your time and work to make thin, even spiral all the way down the potato.

Gently fan out each potato down the length of the skewer, until you have even gaps between the slices. Brush the melted butter all over the potatoes.in a medium bowl add the ingredients.place each skewered potatos over the bowl and sprinkle half of the spices mixture over each potato util they are entirely coated. carefully set the potatoes on a baking pan,leting the skewers rest on sides of the pan so that the potatoes are suspended. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until nicely browned. Garnish with additional Parmesan and parsley. Let cool for 5 minutes before serving.
